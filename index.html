About Green Goddess Collective

Green Goddess Collective is a pioneer in the cannabis industry — one of the original retail locations in Los Angeles and across the nation. For over a decade, we've proudly served the Venice community as a truly unique dispensary, connecting patients and recreational users alike with the finest cannabis products California has to offer.

Our vision is simple: the highest-quality curated products delivered by the most knowledgeable staff in the industry. Cannabis is inherently local, and every product we carry is known fully, from seed to shelf. We don't sell anything we haven't personally evaluated and used ourselves. That's a promise we've kept since day one — and one we'll never compromise on.

Whether you're a California medical cannabis patient managing a condition, or a well-versed recreational user seeking your next great experience, Green Goddess Collective is here to help. We invite you to come in, take your time, ask all the questions you want, and become part of our growing family. At Green Goddess, you're not just a customer — you're family.

Explore the Venice Neighborhood

Green Goddess Collective sits at the heart of one of LA's most vibrant and iconic communities. While you're visiting us, take time to explore everything Venice and the surrounding area has to offer — there's always something happening!

Venice Beach Ocean Front Walk

The iconic boardwalk is steps from our door — street performers, vendors, and endless good vibes await you along the famous promenade.

Muscle Beach

The legendary outdoor gym where bodybuilding culture was born. Watch elite athletes train in the open air, or join a workout yourself!

Venice Skate Park

One of the most famous skate parks in the world, right on the beach. Grab a front-row seat and watch some of California's best skaters do their thing.

Abbot Kinney Blvd Shopping

Boutiques, galleries, coffee shops, and world-class restaurants line this beloved Venice strip. A perfect afternoon adventure after your dispensary visit.

Santa Monica Pier & Shopping District

Just up the coast, Santa Monica's iconic pier, Third Street Promenade, and Ocean Park neighborhood offer endless things to see, eat, and do.

Marina del Rey

A short drive south brings you to the beautiful Marina del Rey harbor — perfect for a sunset stroll, boat tour, or a waterfront meal after your visit.
